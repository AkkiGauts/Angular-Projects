technology and Uses

sr. no. Technology uses
1 Television Television sets transmit signals over which we can listen to and view audio and visual content. People use television to communicate important messages, advertisements, entertainment and more.
2 Internet Many people consider the internet to be the most popular and powerful communication technology. It allows people from around the world to interact through written messages, as well as audio and video messages.
3 Cell phones Cell phones are a modern improvement over the telephone. With a cell phone, people have the ability to call other phones and have a conversation with people across the world.
4 Computers Computers operate through a rapid pulsing of electrical currents. Their core foundation of computers is binary code, which determines whether there is an electrical current.
5 GPS GPS, or a Global Positioning System, is a piece of technology that can pinpoint locations on earth from satellites in earth's orbit. Using this technology, we can now receive real-time directions, monitor the transit of different objects or record precise time measurements.
